The 5-Phase Engagement Process

A structured sequence that moves from listening to co-designing to closing the loop.

01
Listen First
Sidewalk surveys, business walk-throughs, and the QR code campaign. Collect real data before showing any proposal.
02
Show Your Work
Street-level installation of the parking data map. Publish a "We Heard You" report before any solution is announced.
03
Show the Scenarios
Demo Night with GAMA running live. Online simulation video. Let the community watch each future play out — not just hear about it.
04
Decide Together
Tradeoff workshop with scenario voting and revenue allocation. CB6 presentation. Online survey for residents who can't attend in person.
